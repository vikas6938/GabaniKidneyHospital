Vision Eye Hospital

Vision Eye Hospital was established in 2011 by Dr. Shailesh Agrawal, with the mission of providing specialized tertiary eye care services at affordable prices across the city. Vision Eye Hospital is situated in the heart of the city-parle point, and our other clinic is at Parvat Patia.

Every single individual working in Vision Eye Hospital – from doctors to nurses and the management – is united in the endeavor to deliver top-quality eye care to all. We have access to the best diagnosis and latest surgical technology.

We strongly believe in giving patient satisfaction according to their lifestyle and individual needs.
